About CREONIX '25

CREONIX '25 is the flagship Artificial Intelligence and Data Science hackathon of Easwari Engineering College, bringing together the brightest innovators, coders, and problem-solvers. Over 24 hours, participants will tackle real-world challenges, collaborate in teams, and showcase their creativity in building impactful solutions.
